# Interview Session Workflow

This workflow describes the end-to-end process for a candidate to participate in an AI-powered interview.

1. **Access the Platform**
   - Navigate to `https://localhost:8000/register` (or `https://<YOUR_IP>:8000/register`).
   - Create a new candidate account.

2. **Login**
   - Navigate to `https://localhost:8000/login`.
   - Log in with your new credentials.

3. **Join a Room**
   - On the Candidate Dashboard, enter a valid **Room Code** and **Password** (provided by an Admin).
   - Click "Join Interview".

4. **Interview Setup**
   - Upload your resume (PDF) and provide any relevant context.
   - Click "Start Interview".
   - Allow Camera and Microphone permissions.

5. **Conduct Interview**
   - For each question:
     - Read the AI-generated question.
     - Type your answer or use the "Record Answer" button.
     - Review the feedback provided by the AI.
     - Click "Next Question".

6. **Complete Interview**
   - After the final question, click "Return to Dashboard".
   - Verify that your history shows the completed session.
